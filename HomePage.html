The Enchanting World of Books and Libraries: Nurturing the Spirit of Exploration and Knowledge

Books and libraries are the lifeblood of knowledge, weaving a tapestry of wisdom that transcends time. In the grand repository of human understanding, libraries stand as majestic sanctuaries, each book a treasure waiting to be discovered. The written word, a silent guide, beckons the curious to embark on intellectual voyages. Within the hallowed halls of libraries, the scent of aged paper and the whisper of turning pages create an atmosphere of reverence. Books, like portals to different realms, offer escape, enlightenment, and the chance to walk in the shoes of countless characters. In the quietude of a library, minds synchronize with the rhythm of pages being turned, fostering a communion between the reader and the written word.

STEM – Modern educational method being applied in many developed countries.

STEM education is a curriculum based on the idea of equipping learners with knowledge and skills related to the fields of science, technology, engineering and mathematics – in an interdisciplinary approach and learners can apply it to solve problems in everyday life.
STEM is not a subject about scientific knowledge but a method to develop skills:
- Increase the ability to apply knowledge into practice
- Form and develop problem-solving capacity for learners.
- Stimulates creativity and thinking
